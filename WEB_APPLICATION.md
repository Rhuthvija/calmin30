# 🌸 CalmIn30 – AI-Powered Student Wellness & Productivity Platform

🔗 **Live Demo:** https://calmin30-m7o.caffeine.xyz/#grounding

---

## ✨ Overview

**CalmIn30** is an AI-assisted web application designed to support students during moments of stress, anxiety, and reduced focus.

The platform combines evidence-based calming techniques with **FocusIQ**, a machine learning model that analyzes study and lifestyle habits to estimate a user's **Focus Score** and **Procrastination Risk**.

Together, CalmIn30 and FocusIQ provide both **immediate emotional support** and **long-term productivity insights**.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

CalmIn30 was designed to be more than a productivity application.

The goal was to create a **safe digital environment** where students can quickly regulate stress, regain focus, and build healthier study habits through a calming user experience.
